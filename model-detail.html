    Overview

    The 765LT Spider represents the pinnacle of open-top supercar engineering. Born from McLaren's Longtail heritage, it delivers an exhilarating combination of lightweight construction, aerodynamic excellence, and breathtaking performance. With a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing 755 horsepower, this convertible masterpiece accelerates from 0-60 mph in just 2.7 seconds while the retractable hardtop lets you experience every decibel of its symphonic exhaust note.

    Every element has been optimized for track-focused performance without sacrificing the visceral thrill of open-air driving. Carbon fiber construction, active aerodynamics, and race-derived suspension ensure that the 765LT Spider is as capable on the circuit as it is breathtaking on the open road.

    Key Features

    Active Aerodynamics

    Advanced active rear wing and front splitter automatically adjust to optimize downforce and reduce drag.

    Carbon Fiber Monocoque

    Lightweight carbon fiber chassis provides exceptional rigidity and safety while minimizing weight.

    Retractable Hardtop

    One-piece carbon fiber roof retracts in just 11 seconds, even while driving at speeds up to 31 mph.

    Proactive Chassis Control II

    Advanced suspension system with interconnected hydraulic dampers for optimal handling dynamics.

    Carbon Ceramic Brakes

    Race-spec carbon ceramic discs provide fade-free stopping power even under the most demanding conditions.

    Variable Drift Control

    Adjustable stability system allows drivers to fine-tune oversteer characteristics to their preference.
